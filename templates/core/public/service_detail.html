Frequently Asked Questions
How long does a car service take?
A standard car service typically takes 3–5 hours. For a comprehensive service with OBD scan and additional checks, it may take up to 6–7 hours. We'll give you a time estimate when we pick up your car and keep you updated throughout.
Do you use genuine spare parts?
Yes, always. We use OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) or manufacturer-approved parts for all repairs and servicing. You'll receive a digital invoice listing every part used with its brand and price — full transparency guaranteed.
Is pickup and drop really free?
Yes — 100% free for all services booked through eGarage. Our driver will pick up your car from your home or office, take it to our certified garage, and return it once the service is complete. No extra charges at all.
What does the 1000-Day Warranty cover?
The 1000-Day Warranty covers all parts replaced and the labour done during your service. If any issue arises related to our service within 1000 days, we'll re-do the work at no extra cost. This applies to all packages across all services.
Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?
Yes. You can cancel or reschedule up to 2 hours before your scheduled pickup time at no charge. Cancellations made after that may incur a small ₹99 convenience fee. Just call our support team or manage it through the app.
How do I track my car during servicing?
After booking, you'll receive an SMS with a tracking link. You can follow every step of your service in real-time — pickup confirmed, service started, inspection photos, service completed, and car dispatched for return. You can also call our team anytime.
